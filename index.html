FAQs for Photography Safari in Kenya

Do I need a visa for Kenya?

Yes, most travelers need a visa, which can be obtained online via the Kenya eVisa portal.

What is the best time for a photographic safari?

July to October for the wildebeest migration, or January to March for better light conditions.

What photography equipment should I bring?

DSLR/Mirrorless camera, telephoto lens (at least 200mm), wide-angle lens, extra batteries, and memory cards.
